Walnut Drop Earrings

Cut from a single piece of black walnut, these drop earrings weigh almost nothing and catch the light in the most surprising ways. The grain runs vertically through each piece, so no two pairs are ever the same.

Finished with food-safe beeswax and fitted with sterling silver hooks. Available in three lengths.
