I'm Shujauddin — a hobbyist carpenter based in Hyderabad who found peace in the rhythm of wood and blade. What started as a weekend escape became a language of its own: quiet, purposeful, and enduring.

My work centers on everyday objects reimagined in wood — pieces that sit naturally on your desk, hang gently from your ears, or hold the tools of your daily life with grace.
