About the Workshop

Where every cut is deliberate

The workshop started as curiosity — one chisel, one piece of walnut, a lot of wrong cuts. Now it's a small, focused practice making pieces that live in your space and last for years.

Everything is made by hand in Hyderabad, using traditional joinery and sustainable timber. No mass production, no shortcuts — just material, tools, and time.
